My experience in college so far

by: Paulina Guadalupe

When I first got to City Tech, I had started as a Computer Information Systems major but I realized I wouldn't be able to thrive in that field for several reasons, I needed some form of a creative outlet and I came upon the Communication Design department. I've been in this major for the past couple semesters and I feel like I made the right choice. Despite this, I'm still not sure what direction I actually want to take my career in.
No matter what I do in the future, what I aspire to do is be able to work on something that I am truly passionate about and something that i can find fulfilling.

If you cannot do great things, do small things in a great way
